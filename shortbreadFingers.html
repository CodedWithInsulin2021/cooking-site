Ingredients List:

  1. pinch of salt
  2. 100 gr granulated sugar
  3. 200 gr cold butter
  4. 100 gr self-raising flour
  5. 200 gr flour

RECIPE TO MAKE SHORTBREAD FINGER

Put all ingredients in a bowl and cut the butter in the bowl into small pieces with 2 knives or a dough mixer and mix well with the flour and flour. You can of course also do this in a food processor if you have one. Then quickly knead it into a dough with a cool hand. Do not knead too long, a slightly crumbly dough is fine. By kneading too much you lose the airy structure and you will later press the dough out by hand into a flat cake.

Form the sheet of baking paper neatly into the baking tin as a kind of insert and then press the dough into it. Work quickly because the butter is not supposed to soften or even melt. Put the baking pan in the fridge for 1 hour to stiffen

Remove the baking pan from the refrigerator and place it in the middle of a conventional oven preheated to 160ÂºC. After 30 minutes you take out the baking tin and quickly cut the cake (in the baking tin) into 8 strips which you then cut into three again. Stamp the characteristic shortbread fingers pattern with the back of the skewer in each biscuit and put the baking tin back in the oven and bake for another 30 minutes. Remove the shortbread fingers from the baking tin and let it cool on a cooling grid. Once cooled, you can keep the cookies airtight.

Enjoy your meal!
